1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how .gg/FastFlag ("the Bot", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the FastFlag Discord bot.

2. Information We Collect

The Bot may collect and store the following data:

  • Discord user IDs
  • Discord server (guild) IDs
  • Command usage data
  • FastFlag configurations and preferences
  • Error logs related to bot functionality

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly to:

  • Provide and maintain the Bot’s features
  • Store server and user preferences
  • Improve performance and reliability
  • Detect abuse, spam, or misuse
  • Debug issues and prevent errors

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data.

Your data is never shared with third parties except when required by law or to comply with Discord or Roblox policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, including:

  • Restricted access to stored information
  • Secure hosting environments
  • Limited data retention

However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide the Bot’s services.

Data may be automatically deleted when no longer required or when the Bot is removed from a server.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about stored data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Remove the Bot from your server at any time

8. Children’s Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Third-Party Services

The Bot relies on Discord and Roblox APIs. Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Bot means you accept the updated policy.
